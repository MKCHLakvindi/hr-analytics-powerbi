# HR Analytics Dashboard - Power BI
Power BI dashboard analyzing employee attrition and HR metrics

<h2>Overview</h2>
This project presents a comprehensive HR Analytics Dashboard developed using Power BI to analyze workforce data, identify attrition patterns, and support data-driven HR decision-making.
The dashboard transforms raw employee data into meaningful insights that help organizations improve employee retention, optimize workforce planning, and enhance overall HR strategy.

<h2>Business Problem</h2>
<p>Employee attrition is a critical challenge for organizations, leading to increased recruitment costs, knowledge loss, and reduced productivity.
This project aims to answer key business questions:
<p>•	Which departments have the highest attrition?
<p>•	What age groups are most likely to leave?
<p>•	How does job satisfaction impact employee turnover?
<p>•	Are there demographic patterns behind attrition?

<h2>Key Insights</h2>
<p>•	Overall Attrition Rate: 14.57% (58 out of 398 employees)
<p>•	Highest Attrition Department: Research & Development (55%+ contribution)
<p>•	Most Affected Age Group: 25–44 years
<p>•	Job Satisfaction Link: Lower satisfaction levels correlate with higher attrition
<p>•	Gender-Based Trends: Noticeable variation in attrition across age categories

<h2>Dashboard Features</h2>
<p>•	KPI Cards: Total Employees, Attrition Count, Attrition Rate, Active Employees, Average Age
<p>•	Department-wise Attrition Analysis (Pie Chart)
<p>•	Age Group Distribution by Gender (Stacked Bar Chart)
<p>•	Job Satisfaction Matrix (Tabular Heatmap)
<p>•	Education Field-wise Attrition (Bar Chart)
<p>•	Attrition Rate by Gender & Age Group (Donut Charts)

<h2>Business Value</h2>
<p>This dashboard enables HR managers and decision-makers to:
<p>•	Identify high-risk employee segments
<p>•	Design targeted retention strategies
<p>•	Improve employee satisfaction initiatives
<p>•	Support strategic workforce planning

<h2>Tools & Technologies</h2>
<p>•	Power BI – Data visualization & dashboard development
<p>•	Microsoft Excel / CSV – Data source
<p>•	Data Modeling & DAX – KPI calculations and measures
